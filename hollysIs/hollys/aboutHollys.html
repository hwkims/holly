Away from ordinary
The art of creating the joy of
coffee through “small differences”

HOLLYS knows that “the difference between ordinary and extraordinary is just that little “extra””.

Beans grown from different soil and climate keep each different secret filled with each different flavor and aroma. Roasting green beans and preparing espresso might seem like a simple and ordinary process. However, the small and subtle differences during the roasting or extracting process can have a huge impact to the taste.

HOLLYS strives to bring you the “joy of coffee” by revealing the secrets of different beans and letting you enjoy precious moments of having a cup of coffee together with your loved ones.

It is possible because HOLLYS does not consider coffee as a mere tool to quench thirst but approaches coffee as an art. HOLLYS has been continuously exerting its utmost efforts in learning more and more about the various areas of coffee cultivation in the world and in developing quality blends that can accentuate the natural flavors of beans.

HOLLYS will always make extra efforts to create a small meaningful difference to deliver the joy of coffee to more of our customers.

First, the joy in getting to know coffee better

Just as every person is unique, every coffee bean has its own unique characteristics. Skilled baristas delicately extract coffee distinguishing the differences among the beans based on the origin and understanding the customers' needs. As a result, they can provide the biggest joy out of the customers’ coffee experience.

Since founded in 1998, HOLLYS the first Korean coffee company specialized in espresso, has been leading the coffee industry to create the joy of coffee. At the HOLLYS Research Center, licensed Q Graders certified by the Coffee Quality Institute carefully select quality green beans through cupping tests. The HOLLYS Roasting Center develops various roasting profiles based on different origins and blends of the beans.

Also, HOLLYS is committed to delivering the natural flavor of coffee beans into a cup through its special roasting techniques and a double shot method. Hence, HOLLYS raises the value of a cup of coffee in our day-to-day life. In 2011, for the first time in the industry, HOLLYS opened a barista academy designed for many coffee enthusiasts and future entrepreneurs. Since then, it has been contributing to building a sophisticated café culture.

It is easy to gain knowledge but it takes a fair amount of time to gain wisdom. HOLLYS has been sincerely pursuing one path since 1998 and by doing this, HOLLYS could gain the wisdom about coffee, which is our precious asset that nothing could compete with.

Second, the joy in sharing moments with coffee

At HOLLYS , you can enjoy happy moments in your daily life along with your friends, partners, and family, as well as spend some quality time alone.

To achieve this, HOLLYS aims high to create a value beyond comfortable atmosphere. HOLLYS has been widening its horizon by becoming a place where you can enjoy art and culture through public lecture programs such as “School in Hollys.” At the book café in Hapjeong you can gain access to a wide range of books, and at the cafés on the Dalmaji Hill in Busan and on the Bobul Road in Gyeongju you can enjoy the unusual landscape in perfect harmony with its natural surroundings. These are a few example you can have a glimpse at HOLLYS’ will and passion to evolve.

In addition to creating special café spaces, HOLLYS is also proud of its selection of desserts made from quality ingredients such as 100% domestic sweet potatoes and citrons, Madagascan vanilla, and Belgian pearl sugar. All ingredients are managed as carefully as HOLLYS’ coffee beans. Because HOLLYS takes into account the harmony between desserts and beverages from the early stage of developing its menu, you can enjoy a lot richer flavor when you have them together.
HOLLYS will never stop taking extra efforts to bring its customers the best joy and extraordinary experience that coffee can offer.
